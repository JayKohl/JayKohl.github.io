Introduction

Welcome to my personal page! I am Jonathan Kohl, a passionate game developer with a knack for creativity and problem-solving.

Career

My career journey has been exciting and rewarding. Prior to getting a degree in Game Development, I was an EMT and Navy Corpsman. I have worked on various projects as a student at Full Sail University and am currently developing indie games. Here's a brief overview of my professional experience:

  • Active Duty Corpsman - United States Navy (2017-2021): During my time on active duty in the Navy, I worked in the Emergency department at Walter Reed National Military Medical Center. I worked both in the main side emergency department and in the fast track zone. As a senior Corpsman on the floor, I created educational material and held regular trainings on emergency medicine. I was also assigned to the code team as the lead Corpsman/medic. I was a nationally registered EMT, with certifications in Basic Life Saving, Advanced Life Saving, Pediatric Advanced Life Saving, and advanced electrocardiogram reading.
  • Reserve Corpsman - United States Navy (2021-2023): As a reservist, I led funeral details for active duty service members and veterans. I also held trainings for the medical reservists in emergency medicine, TCCC, and electrocardiogram reading. I maintained the medical readiness for the entire Naval reserve center and overlooked clinic supplies. Prior to leaving the Navy Reserve, I earned Sailor of the Quarter at Naval Reserve Center Augusta.

Projects

Over the years, I have worked on several projects that I'm proud of. Here are some highlights:

  • Valarius: Valarius is a sci-fi first-person action shooter. The player is stranded on an alien planet and must endure the hostile inhabitants to complete their mission. During this project, I was in a team of five during portfolio two and three at Full Sail University.
  • In Plain Sight: In Plain Sight is a first-person stealth RPG. The player possesses random NPC around a medieval village. Your goal is to collect ritual items to conduct a cleansing ritual before the villagers all fall prey to the demon.
  • Monster Eater: Monster Eater is a first-person action RPG. The player is a prisoner in hell who is trying to find an escape. You must traverse three levels that are randomly generated to finally defeat the evil mage that has imprisoned you.
